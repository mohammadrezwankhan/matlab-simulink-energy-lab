# MATLAB Simulink Energy Lab

Reproducible MATLAB and Simulink examples for batteries, converters, power electronics, and renewable energy systems.

## Goals

- Keep examples small enough to inspect.
- Document assumptions before results.
- Use clear plots, units, and validation notes.
- Connect simulations to practical engineering questions.
